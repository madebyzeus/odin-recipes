Tacos

Description

These tacos are delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup tomatillos
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup lettuce
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup avocado

Steps

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Cook the corn
  3. Cook the tomatillos
  4. Cook the cheese
  5. Cook the salsa
  6. Cook the sour cream
  7. Cook the lettuce
  8. Cook the onion
  9. Cook the avocado
