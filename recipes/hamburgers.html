Hamburgers

Description

"When you're hungry, eat a burger."

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup mustard
  • 1/2 cup pickles
  • 1/2 cup relish
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup lettuce
  • 1/2 cup tomato
  • 1/2 cup onion

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Shape into hamburger shape.
  4. Grill on a hot pan.
