This is a lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1/2 cup basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, bread crumbs, milk, butter, cheese, mozzarella, parmesan, and basil.
  3. Pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
